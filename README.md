## Airline Market Analysis — Flight Price Intelligence

An interactive Tableau dashboard analysing 10,000+ flight records to uncover 
pricing trends, route performance and airline scheduling patterns across 
Indian domestic routes.
[View Live Dashboard](https://public.tableau.com/views/AirlineMarketAnalysis-FlightPriceIntelligence/ComprehensiveFlightAnalysis)

## Overview

This project analyses the Flight Price Prediction dataset to help travel 
coordinators make cost-efficient, data-driven booking decisions. The dashboard 
answers 5 key business questions through interactive visualisations.

---

## Business Questions Answered

1. Which airline is most frequently scheduled?
2. Which day of the week has the costliest bookings?
3. How did flight counts vary across routes from March–June 2019?
4. How many stops do top airlines take and what is their average price?
5. Which airlines offer the best value on key routes under budget constraints?

---

## Key Insights

- **Jet Airways** leads with 3,849 scheduled flights — highest market share
- **Monday** is the cheapest day to fly at ₹8,741 average — 11% cheaper than Friday (₹9,782)
- **SpiceJet** offers best value on Chennai–Kolkata, Kolkata–Bangalore and Bangalore–Delhi routes
- **Cochin** saw the highest flight count growth from March to June (1,884 flights)
- **Air India** averages the most stops per journey (5) — highest among top carriers

---

## Dashboard Features

| Chart | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Bar Chart | Most booked airlines by flight count |
| Treemap | Average price by day of week |
| Line Graph | Flight count by source/destination March–June |
| Pie Chart | Average stops and price per airline |
| Bar Chart (Complex) | Route-specific airline comparison with filters |

**Interactive filters:** Airline, duration, number of stops, price range, destination

---

## Dataset

- **Source:** [Kaggle — Flight Price Prediction](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/muhammadbinimran/flight-price-prediction)
- **Size:** 10,000+ records
- **Key columns:** Airline, Source, Destination, Route, Dep_Time, Duration, Total_Stops, Price
